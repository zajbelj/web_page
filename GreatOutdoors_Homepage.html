Welcome

The Great Outdoors is dedicated to those who love nature and would like to be involved in many outdoor activities including hiking, walking, bicycling, skiing, snowshoeing, sailing, kayaking, camping and all types of seasonal sports. The purpose of this club is to promote fitness and healthy life-styling in Outdoor Club members and to actively support the concepts of environmental protection.
